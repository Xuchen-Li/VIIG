Designing more human-like tasks.

Utilizing Visual Object Tracking (VOT) as a representative task to explore dynamic visual abilities. VOT holds a pivotal role in computer vision; however, its original definition imposes excessive constraints that hinder alignment with human dynamic visual tracking abilities. To address this problem, We adopt a humanoid modeling perspective and expanded the original VOT definition.
